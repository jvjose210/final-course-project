Welcome to TasteSaipan

TasteSaipan is a simple website that helps you find different kinds of restaurants around the island. Browse through hidden gems and familiar hotspots, and use this site to get ideas for your next meal.

Restaurants on Saipan

The Restaurants page gives a quick look at different places to eat, including what kind of food they serve and about how much you might spend. It is not a full list of every restaurant, just some personal recommendations.

Popular Cuisines

The Cuisines page groups Saipan restaurants by type of food, such as Chamorro, Japanese, Korean, Filipino, and more. You can use it to see common types of food on the island and where you might try them.

Featured Video

The video shows a few examples of the foods you can experience when eating out on Saipan. Watch it for a quick preview before exploring the rest of the site.
